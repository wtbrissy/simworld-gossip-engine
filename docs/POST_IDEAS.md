## Medium opening

Most AI writing starts with a prompt.

Mine started with a town.

I did not want another chatbot. A chatbot waits for me to speak. It responds, then waits again. Useful, but not alive.

So I built a tiny simulated town on my mini PC. It had 100 residents with jobs, homes, goals, secrets, stress, health, relationships, and memories. Every day, the town advanced by itself. People changed. Secrets stayed hidden or began to leak. Some characters became more important than others.

The AI did not invent the story from nothing.

It wrote from a world that had already moved.
